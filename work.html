Work Shop

Title: Exploring the World of Computer Science>

>Duration: 2-3 hours (adjust according to audience and depth) Objective: Introduce participants to fundamental computer science concepts, programming, and real-world applications.

Section 1: Introduction (15 minutes)

1. What is Computer Science?

2. Brief history and impact

3. Career opportunities

Section 2: Programming Basics (45 minutes)

1. Introduction to coding ( Scratch, Python, or Blockly)

2. Variables, data types, loops, and conditionals

3. Hands-on coding exercise

Section 3: Web Development (45 minutes)

1. HTML/CSS basics

2. Building a simple web page

3. Interactive web development exercise

Materials:

1. Computers/laptops/tablets

2. Internet connection

3. Projector/screen

4. Whiteboard/markers

5. Printed or digital workshop materials
